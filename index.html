About the magazine

Bettering utilises the artistic talent of residents and friends to raise money and awareness for important issues. It encompasses everything Manor Hall is known for; it uses Manor's privilege to help those who are less privileged.

This first issue is raising money for the refugee crisis. All profits go to Bristol Refugee Rights, an organisation that seeks to tackle an international crisis right here in Bristol.

By supporting Manor Hall's magazine Bettering you strengthen Manor Hall's community, and use that unity to help others.
